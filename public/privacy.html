Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2025

Summary: We collect only your email address to provide access to our presentation and for relevant business communications. We use Microsoft Clarity for analytics to improve user experience. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Email Address: Required to access our presentation content
  • Usage Analytics: Through Microsoft Clarity, including page views, clicks, and navigation patterns
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device information, and IP address (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Providing access to our presentation content
  • Sending relevant business communications and updates
  • Analyzing and improving our website performance and user experience
  • Understanding our audience to create better content

3. Microsoft Clarity Analytics

We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how you interact with our website. Clarity may record:

  • Mouse movements, clicks, and scrolling behavior
  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • General geographic location (country/region level)

Clarity does not collect personally identifiable information beyond what we explicitly provide. Learn more about Microsoft's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your email address is stored locally in your browser and on Microsoft's secure servers through Clarity. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of communications (unsubscribe links provided in emails)

6. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to remember your email address so you don't need to re-enter it on subsequent visits. This information is stored locally on your device and can be cleared through your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Microsoft Clarity: For website analytics and user experience insights

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
